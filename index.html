Skysaver Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Skysaver. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with Skysaver, an AI-powered assistant designed to find the best flight fares and plan travel.

2. Information Collection

When you use Skysaver, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, etc.
  • Travel Information: Travel dates, destinations, preferences, etc.
  • Interaction Data: Queries to and interactions with Skysaver.

3. Use of Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide personalized travel suggestions and fare comparisons.
  • Improve Skysaver's functionality and user experience.
  • Communicate with users for service-related purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your information may be shared with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers for flight data and booking services.
  • Legal entities, if required by law.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information, including encryption and secure server hosting.

6. Use of AI Tools

Skysaver uses AI tools like DALL-E, a browser, and Python programming for data processing. These tools are governed by their respective privacy policies and terms of service.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of certain data uses.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
